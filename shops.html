Mali Sweet Shop Mali Sweet Shop is one of the oldest and most trusted sweet shops in Sihor. Located near the central market, this shop is famous for Peda, Barfi, Mohanthal, and traditional Gujarati sweets. Many people, especially locals and visitors from nearby villages, prefer Mali shop for its authentic taste and pure quality. It is especially crowded during festivals and weddings. The shop has maintained its traditional taste over the years and is considered a landmark in Sihor.

Muni Sweet Shop Muni Sweet Shop is another very popular and fast-growing sweet store in Sihor. It is known for its variety of snacks like Fafda-Jalebi, Khaman, Dhokla, and Sev-Khamni. The shop is modern in appearance and attracts the youth more because of its clean setup and fast service. It is also known for its tasty fresh breakfast-time snacks and evening farsan. While it is not as old as Mali shop, it is very famous among the younger crowd and people who prefer light snacks over traditional mithai.

While both shops are popular, the Mali Sweet Shop is considered more traditional and respected, especially among older generations and people looking for pure, festive sweets for puja, bhog, and weddings. On the other hand, Muni Sweet Shop is very popular among college students and office-goers for its snack items and quick service. So, Mali is more famous for traditional sweets, and Muni is famous for snacks and fast food-style sweets.
